Proc Details

proc
generate_masked_leg
(/image/limb_overlay) as /list

This proc serves as a way to ensure that legs layer properly on a mob. To do this, two separate images are created - A low layer one, and a normal layer one. Each of the image will appropriately crop out dirs that are not used on that given layer.

Arguments:

  • limb_overlay - The limb image being masked, not necessarily the original limb image as it could be an overlay on top of it Returns the list of masked images, or null if the limb_overlay didn't exist
