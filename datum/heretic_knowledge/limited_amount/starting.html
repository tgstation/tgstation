starting
/datum/heretic_knowledge/limited_amount/starting

A knowledge subtype for limited_amount knowledge used for base knowledge (the ones that make blades) Grants your path-relevant grasp upgrade, passive and grasp mark

A heretic can only learn one /starting type knowledge, and their ascension depends on whichever they chose.
