2014.06.20

Pomf123 updated:

  • Big tweaks to how some goon genetics abilities work.
  • Stealth is now applied over time instead of being instant.
  • Cryokinesis damage nerf, now respects space worthy suits + internals.
  • Trying to leap while restrained or pinned will result in you getting slammed back on the ground.
  • T-Ray scanners and flashes will reveal invisible mobs for a short period of time. This includes vampires and genetic invisibility.

2014.06.19

Cloroxygen updated:

  • Added High Energy Particle Scatterer: Makes it way easier to deal with tumors.
    IMPORTANT NOTE: The H.E.P.S. leaks a small amount of radiation when the beam is activated (much like a cobalt beam or xray irl) so be sure to close the oncology shutters and wear your oncology lab coat!
    ANOTHER IMPORTANT NOTE:The H.E.P.S. map placement has been delayed so it may not appear in every round.

2014.06.17

Cloroxygen updated:

  • Added oncology: This includes cancer, oncologist job, oncologist's office, chemo, chemo IV pack, oncologist wardrobe, oncology pamphlet (which explains how to cure cancer), and oncologist lab coat.

    TO AVOID GETTING CANCER:
    Do not linger with toxins in your body.
    There is now a toxin threshold and exposure time threshold...having elevated toxins in your blood for several minutes can/will cause cancer. It now kills more slowly than in the several test rounds you've experienced it in until now. Also: If your supply of chemo runs out as oncologist, more can be made with Arithrazine, Diethylamine, and Unstable Mutagen. More updates planned for this addon.

  • All objects caught in explosions or in Singuloth's maw are now deleted using a new technique. This should make explosions and singulo releases nearly lagless.

After the gun is out, you can insert a new item, and lock the display case by swiping your ID. Breaking it requires harm intent, otherwise it's the same as the old display case.
  • Display cases are constructable by using a glass sheet on an empty machine frame, then inserting airlock controller board and screwdrivering it. Permissions set on airlock controller will be set on the display case.
  • Injector flow rate is now controllable from General Air Control consoles.
  • Added planning frame to RD's office. Allows you to plan out a lawset by inserting boards into it, examine to preview, right-click > dry run to check for common mistakes. Runs all laws at once when inserted an upload.
  • Clicking on the name of a design in a circuit imprinter or protolathe will build it immediately.
  • Genetics has a vast new array of powers and disabilities, and nearly twice as many blocks to contend with.
  • Bartender and Detective now start off with the Sober gene.
  • Overdoses treatable with Activated Charcoal, available via Biogenerator. Must be ground and then injected or ingested.
  • People who fail to spawn with their items (e.g. fatties) will now get them in a plastic bag held in their hand.
  • Due to abuse, mice are now killed by tasers and other projectiles.
  • Magboots can be stored in Suit Storage Units.
  • Assistant jumpsuit is now fat-compatible.
  • Jumpsuits with missing sprites show up with an ugly black and purple checkerboard pattern.

  • Silver slimes now produce drinks upon injection with water.
  • Flames now properly extinguish.
  • Bats are now a swarm of bats.
  • Slimes split from a killed adult will be able to understand humans.
