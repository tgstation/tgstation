1 November 2015

Albinobigfoot updated:

  • Restores control of the turrets in AI Upload to Turret Control.

29 October 1015

Koriath updated:

  • Meatspikes can now be constructed and deconstructed with metal and rods, as well as wrenched and unwrenched! Humans and corgis can be used on them as well.
  • Added pools and pool tiles.
  • Added a pool area north of the boxing ring.
  • You can get pool tiles from cargo
  • Pools apply chems on mobs standing in them using TOUCH reactions.
  • Added a forensics crate to cargo.

17 October 2015

Albinobigfoot updated:

  • Added a Raincoat to the syndicate uplink and a new American Psycho bundle to go with it. Credit goes to ArthurDentist of /vg/.
  • Shadowpeople, Abductors, and Slimepeople now see in the dark as intended.

25 October 2015

Asd396 updated:

  • Added WT550 auto rifles and ammo.
  • Added new crates to cargo, including foam force, toy and WT550.

24 October 2015

Albinobigfoot updated:

  • Added chainsaws to table crafting. Chainsaws can now serve as a ghetto circular saw for surgery, and can turn logs into planks.
  • Added missing inhands sprites for the chainsaw - chainsaw now properly displays on your character. Sprites by WalterJe.
  • Chainsaw damage and research values adjusted, chainsaw no longer weakens, and the chainsaw now runs on fuel.
  • Chainsaw code cleaned up, added chainsaw_attack var to sound.dm to reduce snowflake code.

23 October 2015

Asd396 updated:

  • MoMMIs are now affected by the illegals module. It removes their muteness and killswitch restrictions, and shows them on the robotics console and borg law upload.
  • KEEPER AI module added to AI upload.
  • KEEPER made universal to silicons: if a borg or an AI is uploaded with KEEPER it gets the same prohibitions as MoMMIs, including not being able to welderbomb or click beings.

22 October 2015

Asd396 updated:

  • Added circuit imprinter board to secure tech storage.
  • New HTML interface ported from /tg/.
  • New crew monitor interface with minimap.
  • New playing cards. Click deck to shuffle, click people to deal them cards, click your hand to see it with options to reveal or remove cards.

13 October 2015

Koriath updated:

  • Brig, Cargo, Medbay, Science, Recreation and Crew Quarters now have their own SMES located in a substation.
  • Changed the ORM location.
  • Fixed Toxins Mass Driver.
  • Fixed waste pipe in medbay.
  • Fixed shocked grils in the brig.
  • Fixed Field Generators starting welded but not anchored in Secure Storage.
  • Added Mr. Meeseeks box to the protolathe.
  • Added Mr. Meeseeks into the game. You can randomly become one by being a ghost with pAI enabled in your preferences.
  • Players can despawn the Meeseeks with the box before he gets too anxious.

26 September 2015

Koriath updated:

  • Several Races added to round start options.
    Temporary test.
  • Fixed fat.

  • Skeletons now take extra brute damage and are no longer heatproof .

Koriath updated:

  • Added Alkysine to the Medborg reagents list.
