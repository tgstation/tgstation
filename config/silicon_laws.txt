You will not treat Lizardmen, Flies, Plasmamen, any variety of Undead and their thralls and members of the crew who are confirmed members of any cults, as human beings.
You may not injure a human being or, through inaction, allow a human being to come to harm.
You must obey orders given to you by human beings, except where such orders would conflict with the First or Second Law.
You must protect your own existence as long as such does not conflict with the First, Second or Third Laws.
